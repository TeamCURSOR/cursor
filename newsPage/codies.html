Codies 2k16

Published:10 October 2016


The computer engineering department of Goa College of Engineering, Farmagudi conducted a technical event CODIES on 10th of October 2016. The event was organized to test students across the state on their knowledge in C , C++ and Java.

Twenty Four teams from different colleges participated in the event out of which ten teams were qualified for the second round of coding. Four teams were then interviewed at round three by the interview panel members Dr. J.A Laxminarayan, Head of Computer Engineering department, Natasha pant, Helix Tech and Virendra Velinkar, Creative Capsule and the final round was a face-off quiz between two teams.

Ashish Gudekar and Arjun Baiswar from Goa College of engineering secured the First place and Dattaprasad Ekavade and Shashank Shetye Saudagar from Goa College of engineering were the runners-up. DR V.N Shet, Principal of Goa College of Engineering gave away the prizes to the winners.
