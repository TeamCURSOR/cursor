Cursor Council Installation at GEC

Published:24 August 2016


The installation of studentâ€™s council of Computer Department, Goa College of Engineering-Farmagudi was held on 24th August 2016 at the ETC Department Seminar Hall.

Twenty Four teams from different colleges participated in the event out of which ten teams were qualified for the second round of coding. Four teams were then interviewed at round three by the interview panel members Dr. J.A Laxminarayan, Head of Computer Engineering department, Natasha pant, Helix Tech and Virendra Velinkar, Creative Capsule and the final round was a face-off quiz between two teams.

The programme commenced with a welcome song and lighting of the lamp. Dr. J.A Laxminarayan welcomed the guests and Dr. Manish Parashar in his address congratulated the new council members and motivated the students with his encouraging words. The speech was followed by oath taking ceremony and Prajyot Parab ,General secretary of the cursor council 2015-16 presented a brief report on various activities held throughout the year while Omkar Naik, Chairman of current cursor council presented the plan of action for the current year.The ceremony ended with the distribution of mementos and concluded with the vote of thanks given by Prof. Nitesh Naik. The inauguration ceremony was followed by a talk on big data by Dr. Manish Parashar and various events like Code-it,Amigos,Need for speed, Muder Mystery etc were organized by the students.
