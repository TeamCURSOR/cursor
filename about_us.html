What is CURSOR?

CURSOR Council is the Computer Students Organization of

Computer Engineering Department of GEC.



CURSOR was founded with a MOTIVE to promote latest trends

and research challenges in the field of computer science

and engineering by means of CONDUCTING workshops,

invited talks and field trips .



Cursor council has been consistently working for PRMOTING

the latest advancements in the technical as well as

ENTREPRENEURSHIP domain of computer science engineering and IT.
